1. Select Window/Blood Splatter Effect/Create

2. Assign all of your blood splatter sprites to the "Blood Splatter Sprites" array

3. Set a duration - this is how long(in seconds) the effect will last on screen

4. Call BloodSplatterEffect.instance.BloodSplatter(); in your scripts whenever you want a blood splatter effect.

5. Have Fun!
